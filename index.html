About Us

A Journey of
Purpose & Reflection

Maqna.id merupakan historical tour agency dari Bogor yang melakukan perjalanan bermakna ke Negara Islamicity Index seperti New Zealand, serta perjalanan Peradaban dan Sejarah Islam seperti Turki dan Serbia.

Maqna.id is a historical tour agency from Bogor that carries out meaningful trips to the countries of the Islamicity Index such as New Zealand, and Islamic civilization or history destinations such as Turkiye and Serbia. Our mission is to help Muslims reflect on Islamic values, understand global Islamic heritage, and travel with deeper purpose.

New Zealand —
Where Islamic Values
Come to Life

New Zealand offers a unique opportunity to understand how Islamic values such as justice, cleanliness, tolerance, and social harmony are reflected in a non-Muslim country. This journey combines nature, cultural learning, and meaningful reflection.

Participants explore Auckland, Rotorua, Maori communities, and natural landmarks while engaging in guided discussions about Islamic values in modern society — encouraging reflection on how Muslims can implement these values in everyday life.

    About Us

    A Journey of
    Purpose & Reflection

    Maqna.id merupakan historical tour agency dari Bogor yang melakukan perjalanan bermakna ke Negara Islamicity Index seperti New Zealand, serta perjalanan Peradaban dan Sejarah Islam seperti Turki dan Serbia.

    Maqna.id is a historical tour agency from Bogor that carries out meaningful trips to the countries of the Islamicity Index such as New Zealand, and Islamic civilization or history destinations such as Turkiye and Serbia. Our mission is to help Muslims reflect on Islamic values, understand global Islamic heritage, and travel with deeper purpose.

    Deep Dive

    Türkiye —
    Islamic Civilization
    Come to Life

    Türkiye offers a rare opportunity to witness the grandeur of Islamic civilization through the legacy of the Ottoman Empire and the extraordinary leadership of Sultan Muhammad Al-Fatih. This journey combines history, architecture, and meaningful reflection.

    Participants explore Edirne, Istanbul, İznik, Bursa, Cappadocia, and Ankara while engaging in guided discussions about Islamic governance and the historic conquest of Constantinople — one of the greatest achievements in Islamic history.
